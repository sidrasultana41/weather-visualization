Latitude vs. Wind Speed

The majority of the cities were experiencing humidity levels greater than 40-50%. On very humid days the air outside is very stagnant and you hardly feel any type of breeze. Looking at the Wind Speed scatter plot the majority of the cities were experiencing Wind Speeds between 0 to 10 mph.
